What is Logosophy?

      Logosophy is a science created by Carlos Bernardo González Pecotche, by definition “Logos” means the written or spoken word, but Heraclitus believed that it means rationality and a cosmic principle of order and beauty. Yet “Sophia” means the wisdom, or the intelligence. For the author the word means “An original science or wisdom to designate a new line of knowledge, a school, a method and a technique that are eminently their own”.

      The science has as objective the conscious evolution of humankind, it guides us by the author books and the institution's own discoveries.
