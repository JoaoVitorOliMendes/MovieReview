Self Knowledge

      Logosophy is the science of conscious human evolution, this great objective is only accomplished with self knowledge. It’s the first and most important step to overcome yourself, because, logically to overcome your flaws, first, you have to know what they are.

      That’s why we have to seek for more knowledge everyday, study must be constant, as the author said in “Bases for your Conduct”: “Consecrate as much time as possible to study, with faith, enthusiastically, increasing your knowledge and feeling disposed”.

      Making goals is a great way to outgrow, recognize your deficiencies and aspects you want to improve and make a mental plan for how and where to start. It takes time and maestry to get better, no one climbs the Everest in one day, take your time to achieve your goals.
